What makes the Toyota Tundra a great choice for the trails?

Top Features Of The Toyota Tundra For Overlanding:

Here are some of the best assets of this vehicle:

  1. Robust Powertrain

If you’re looking for power, then the Tundra will not let you down. This is one of the areas that you want to make sure that you have more than enough. If you are a complete beginner, you may not have to worry about power quite as much, however we recommend going for a strong engine.

You will find that the Tundra gives you a powerful engine and it also has a strong transmission that means you will be able to carry all of your gear and tackle any trail without any issues.

  1. Reliability

This is a major piece to the puzzle. Any vehicle that you take on the trails should give you something that is reliable. As you will be venturing into isolated parts of the map you need to make sure that the vehicle that you have is reliable and will not leave you stranded. The Tundra gives you a fantastic choice for reliability and peace of mind.

  1. Off-Road Capabilities

The Tundra is also designed for off-road; this means that you should have smooth navigation no matter which terrain you are trying to tackle. You also find that it comes with off-road packages and has features such as skid plates, off-road tyres and the differential lock which means that it should be able to tackle any terrain and allow you to have more fun doing so.

  1. Spacious Interior and Cargo Space

The Tundra is also a fantastic choice if you are going further afield for a longer time. You will find that it gives you a spacious interior and also has ample cargo space so you will be able to store everything from your camping gear to your supplies.

When you pair this with the Trail’d tank you will be saving even more space because it allows you to install the tanks in the spare tyre space. This will keep the vehicle balanced and it also means that you can carry far more water without taking up precious storage space.

  1. Towing Capacity

This is an added extra but a feature that we highly recommend. If you’re looking for a strong towing capability, then you will find that the Tundra will meet your needs here. This is essential if you wish to be able to carry heavy equipment and you should be able to use this to pull a camper, trailer or even extra supplies.
